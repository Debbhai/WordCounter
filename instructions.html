Introduction

Instructions:

  • Typing or Pasting Text : Start typing or paste your content into the large text box.
  • Automatic Updates : The tool will calculate and display the word and character counts as you type or after pasting text.
  • Clear All : Press the "Clear All" button to erase the text and reset the counts.
  • Paste : Press the "Paste" button to insert text from your clipboard directly into the text area.
  • Extending of Text Box : By dragging the text box from the right bottom you can extend it as your preference.
